# Jeeraan (Neighbors) – Product Guidelines

## 1. Voice and Tone
- **Friendly and Community-Oriented:** The tone should be welcoming and professional, fostering a sense of community.
- **Clear and Direct:** Use concise, straightforward language to ensure accessibility for all age groups.
- **Empathetic:** Acknowledge user concerns (e.g., in service orders) with supportive phrasing.

## 2. Branding and Aesthetics
- **Vibrant and Energizing:** Use a balanced color palette that feels modern and "alive."
- **Premium Tactile Feel:** Incorporate subtle noise textures and multi-layered shadows to create depth.
- **Consistency:** Ensure uniform typography (Sans-serif for body, Hero text for headlines) across mobile and web.

## 3. UX Principles
- **Clarity First:** Prioritize clear labels, intuitive navigation, and helpful iconography.
- **Interactive Feedback:** Provide visual "glow" effects and interactive feedback for all buttons and inputs.
- **Accessibility:** Ensure high contrast ratios (WCAG 4.5:1) and support for dynamic text scaling.
- **Responsiveness:** Maintain a seamless experience across mobile phones, tablets, and desktop browsers.

## 4. Design Guidelines
- **Modern Simplicity:** Focus on clean layouts with adequate spacing (whitespace) to prevent clutter.
- **Interactive Visuals:** Use stylized CSS shapes, gradients, and subtle animations to enhance the "feel" of the app.
- **Consistency:** Use consistent patterns for common UI elements like cards, buttons, and input fields.
