# **Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)**

## **General**

**1. What is Jeeraan?**
Jeeraan is a private, neighborhood-based platform that enables residents to communicate, stay informed, report issues, participate in decisions, and engage with their community in a secure digital environment.

**2. Who can use Jeeraan?**
Jeeraan is designed for verified residents of a specific neighborhood or apartment community. Access is granted through invitation by an admin or authorized member.

**3. Is Jeeraan public or private?**
Jeeraan neighborhoods are **private and access-controlled**. Only verified residents can join and view content within their community.

---

## **Account & Access**

**4. How do I join my neighborhood on Jeeraan?**
You must receive a **unique invitation link or code** from your neighborhood admin or inviter. After verification, you can create an account and join.

**5. What if I didn’t receive an invitation?**
You can request access from your neighborhood admin or contact support to verify your eligibility.

**6. Can I belong to multiple neighborhoods?**
Currently, users join a single neighborhood. Multi-neighborhood support may be introduced in future updates.

---

## **Features & Usage**

**7. What can I do on Jeeraan?**
Residents can:

* View and comment on announcements
* Participate in polls and voting
* Report issues and track progress
* Submit and engage with grievances
* Join discussions in forums
* Post or browse classified ads

---

## **Grievances & Issue Reporting**

**8. What is the difference between a work order and a grievance?**

* **Work Orders** are typically individual maintenance requests.
* **Grievances** are **community-visible issues** that may affect multiple residents and allow discussion, tracking, and transparency.

**9. Can other residents see my grievance?**
Yes. Grievances are visible to members of your neighborhood to promote awareness and collaboration.

**10. How do I track the status of my grievance?**
Each grievance includes a status:

* Pending
* In Progress
* Resolved

You will also receive updates through notifications.

**11. Can I add photos to a grievance?**
Yes. Adding images is encouraged to help admins better understand and resolve issues.

---

## **Communication & Engagement**

**12. Will I receive notifications?**
Yes. You may receive **push, email, or SMS notifications** depending on your settings and the urgency of updates.

**13. Can I control what notifications I receive?**
Yes. Notification preferences can be customized within your account settings.

**14. Can I comment on posts and grievances?**
Yes, unless commenting is disabled by the admin for specific content.

---

## **Privacy & Security**

**15. Is my personal information safe?**
Yes. Jeeraan uses secure authentication and encrypted communication to protect user data and complies with privacy regulations such as GDPR and CCPA.

**16. Who can see my information?**
Only verified members of your neighborhood and authorized admins can access your profile information, based on privacy settings.

---

## **Administration**

**17. What can admins do?**
Admins can:

* Manage users and invitations
* Post announcements
* Moderate discussions
* Manage grievances and work orders
* Configure neighborhood settings

**18. Can admins remove content or users?**
Yes. Admins have moderation rights to maintain a safe and respectful environment.

---

## **Classifieds & Community Features**

**19. How do I post an item for sale or giveaway?**
You can create a classified listing with a title, description, optional price, and photos.

**20. Do listings expire?**
Yes. Listings may have an expiration date to keep the marketplace relevant and uncluttered.

---

## **Support & Troubleshooting**

**21. What should I do if I encounter a technical issue?**
You can contact support through the app or website. Future updates may include in-app help and ticketing.

**22. What if incorrect or inappropriate content is posted?**
You can report the content, and admins/moderators will review and take appropriate action.

---

## **Future Enhancements**

**23. Will new features be added to Jeeraan?**
Yes. Planned enhancements include:

* Multi-neighborhood support
* Advanced analytics for admins
* SMS notifications
* Integration with third-party services
